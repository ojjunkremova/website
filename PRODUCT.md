# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Homeowners, renters, landlords, real estate teams, contractors, and small businesses in the Atlanta Metro Area who need junk removed quickly without chasing callbacks. They are often dealing with a move, renovation, garage cleanout, appliance removal, estate cleanout, or cluttered property and want proof that the crew will arrive on time, quote clearly, protect the space, and finish fast.

## Product Purpose

OJ Junk Removal's site converts urgent local visitors into free quote bookings and phone calls. It should communicate same-day availability, 5-star trust, real job proof, eco-conscious disposal, and a simple path to schedule an estimate through Calendly or call (404) 399-7457.

## Brand Personality

Fast, capable, and neighborly. The brand should feel like a sharp local hauling crew with pride in clean work: direct language, visible before-and-after proof, confident CTAs, and a practical service-first tone.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI landing-page polish, fake stock-review layouts, washed-out eco palettes, oversized decorative cards, emoji icons, vague sustainability claims, and slow pages that hide the quote action. Do not make it feel like a SaaS template or a generic national franchise.

## Design Principles

Show the work before asking for trust.

Keep every fold conversion-ready with a phone or quote path.

Use specific Atlanta Metro service language, not broad filler.

Make cleanup feel immediate: fast sections, crisp hierarchy, motion that suggests hauling and clearing without blocking content.

Balance urgency with professionalism so the site feels reliable, not pushy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and keyboard-accessible navigation. Respect reduced-motion preferences, keep phone and quote links semantic, provide descriptive alt text for job images, and avoid relying on color alone for status or emphasis.
